In the rainy season there are a lot of mushroom in our [college area(KIT)](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g729357-d3804229-Reviews-Kirirom_National_Park-Kampong_Speu_Kampong_Speu_Province.html), And when we have free time we went to collect mushroom in the forest but we are not sure which mushroom is dangerous or poisonous. That’s why I try to build a mobile application based on AI that can classify if the mushroom is poisonous by just taking a photo of the mushroom more importantly it can work even in offline mode.
